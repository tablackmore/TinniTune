What this is. A free self-management tool grounded in the research (see the evidence & design document). Sound therapy can offer relief and help you habituate; the evidence for a durable cure is limited, and we say so. What it isn't: a medical device or a substitute for an audiologist or doctor. If your tinnitus is one-sided, pulses with your heartbeat, or comes on suddenly with hearing loss, please see a clinician.
